About

Technical problem-solving through visual storytelling

I am Marco Antonio Mendoza López, an FX Artist Student focused on procedural simulation, 3D production, cinematic storytelling and interactive media. My work explores the connection between technical problem-solving and visual impact, using tools such as Houdini, Maya, Unity and digital production workflows.

I am especially interested in destruction simulations, smoke and particle effects, procedural modeling and cinematic scenes that combine artistic direction with technical execution.
